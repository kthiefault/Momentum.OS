Brand Guidelines v1.0

Momentum.OS

Visual identity, voice, and usage guidelines for Momentum Labs, Inc.

01 — Logo

The Momentum.OS logo consists of the atomic nucleus mark and the wordmark. Use the primary (color) version whenever possible. Reserve white or dark versions for contexts where the color version lacks contrast.

Primary — Color (on dark)
Reversed — White (on dark)
Dark — On light backgrounds
Icon only — App icons, favicons

02 — Logo Usage Rules

✓ Do

Use the color logo on dark backgrounds (#08090C or similar).

✗ Don't

Never stretch, distort, or rotate the logo.

✓ Do

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the ".OS" text on all sides.

✗ Don't

Never place the color logo on a busy photo or pattern without a semi-transparent background overlay.

✓ Do

Use the icon-only mark for app icons, favicons, and small social profile images.

✗ Don't

Never recolor the logo in non-brand colors or apply drop shadows to the wordmark.

05 — Voice & Tone

Momentum.OS speaks like a high-performing operator who's figured it out — not a tech company trying to impress. We're direct, confident, and focused on real outcomes. No fluff.

✓ Write like this

  • Direct and outcome-focused
  • "Stop losing 41 hours a week" — specific, urgent
  • Plain English over jargon
  • Bold claims backed by real numbers
  • Short sentences. High energy.
  • Speak to the reader's pain first

✗ Avoid this

  • Overly technical / buzzword-heavy
  • "Leverage synergistic AI paradigms"
  • Passive voice ("it can be configured to...")
  • Vague promises ("transform your business")
  • Corporate language / hedging
  • More than 2 exclamation points per page
