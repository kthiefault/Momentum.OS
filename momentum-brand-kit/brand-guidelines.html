BRAND GUIDELINES

Visual identity, voice, and usage guidelines for Momentum Labs, Inc.

01 — Logo

Momentum.OS uses two marks: the favicon badge (a gradient-bordered square with a glowing center dot) and the atomic nucleus mark (orbit rings + electrons + nucleus core). Use the badge mark for app icons and profile images; use the atom mark for editorial and display contexts.

Primary — Badge + Wordmark
Atom Mark + Wordmark
Dark — On light backgrounds
Icon variants

02 — Logo Usage Rules

✓ Do

Use the primary logo (badge) on very dark backgrounds (#09090e or similar).

✗ Don't

Never stretch, rotate, or alter the proportions of either mark.

✓ Do

Use the atom icon for editorial, social headers, and large display contexts.

✗ Don't

Never place the white logo on a white or very light background.

✓ Do

Use the favicon badge (32×32 or 64×64) for app icons, browser tabs, and social profile pictures.

✗ Don't

Never recolor the nucleus orbits. They are always white (with varying opacity) on dark, or dark on light.

03 — Color Palette

The Momentum.OS palette is near-monochromatic — deep near-black backgrounds with pure white for all primary marks and text. The only accent color is blue, used sparingly for the favicon gradient and interactive elements.

05 — Voice & Tone

Momentum.OS speaks like a high-performing operator who has figured it out — not a tech company trying to impress. Direct, confident, and focused on real outcomes. No fluff.

✓ Write like this

  • Direct and outcome-focused
  • "Stop losing 41 hours a week" — specific, urgent
  • Plain English over jargon
  • Bold claims backed by real numbers
  • Short sentences. High energy.

✗ Avoid this

  • Overly technical / buzzword-heavy
  • "Leverage synergistic AI paradigms"
  • Passive voice and hedging
  • Vague promises with no numbers
  • Corporate language
